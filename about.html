About Us

A River of Life in the Heart of Bengaluru

River of Life Christian Church is a Spirit-filled, Bible-based church family in Bengaluru. Our desire is to see people encounter Jesus, be rooted in God's Word, and be released to live for His glory.

“There is a river whose streams make glad the city of God.” – Psalm 46:4

Our name reflects our call to be a life-giving river in our city and nation.

Our Story

River of Life Christian Church began as a small gathering of believers hungry for the presence of God and the pure teaching of His Word. Over the years, God has faithfully grown this community into a vibrant church family in Bengaluru.

From the beginning, our heart has been to host the presence of God, make disciples of Jesus, and send believers into every sphere of society as carriers of His life and light. We are grateful for the many testimonies of salvation, healing, restoration, and breakthrough that have come as people have encountered Jesus here.

Today, River of Life Christian Church continues to be a home for people from different languages, backgrounds, and stories – united in Christ, rooted in Scripture, and led by the Holy Spirit.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered, Spirit-filled church that brings the life of Jesus to individuals, families, and nations.

Our Mission

  • • Exalt Jesus through passionate worship and prayer.
  • • Equip believers through systematic, Bible-based teaching.
  • • Establish healthy families and Christ-centered community.
  • • Engage our city and nation with the gospel of grace.

Statement of Faith

River of Life Christian Church joyfully affirms historic, evangelical, Bible-believing Christian doctrine.

The Bible

We believe the Bible – both Old and New Testaments – is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, our final rule for faith and life.

2 Timothy 3:16–17 · 2 Peter 1:20–21

The Godhead

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit – co-equal, co-eternal, and worthy of the same worship.

Deuteronomy 6:4 · Matthew 28:19

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully Man, born of a virgin, sinless in His life, who died on the cross as our substitute, rose bodily from the dead, and will return in glory.

John 1:1–14 · 1 Corinthians 15:3–4

Salvation

We believe that all have sinned and are in need of salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works, but as the gift of God.

Romans 3:23–24 · Ephesians 2:8–9

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells believers, empowers them for holy living, and distributes gifts for the building up of the Church.

Acts 1:8 · 1 Corinthians 12

The Church & Eternity

We believe in the one universal Church, the Body of Christ, and in the reality of eternal life with God for believers and eternal separation from God for those who reject Christ.

Matthew 16:18 · Revelation 20:11–15
