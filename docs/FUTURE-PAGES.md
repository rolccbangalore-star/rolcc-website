## FAQ and Rich Results (SEO)

When building or expanding pages, consider adding **FAQ sections** and **How-to content** so we can mark them up for rich results (FAQ schema, HowTo schema). This helps visibility in search and answers “churches near me” / service-related questions.

- **Where to add FAQ/How-to:** Membership (baptism, wedding, communion), Services (times, languages, what to expect), River Kids (what to bring, age groups), Contact (directions, parking), Giving (how to give, UPI), Counselling (what we offer, how to book).
- **Implementation:** Use a consistent FAQ block (question + answer); add `application/ld+json` FAQPage or HowTo schema when the content is ready. Keep answers concise and keyword-aware (e.g. Bangalore, HSR Layout, service times).
- **Priority pages for FAQ/rich snippets:** Membership, Services, Contact, River Kids.
