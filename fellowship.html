Fellowship

Doing Life Together as a Church Family

Church is more than a Sunday service. Through cell groups and fellowships, we grow in Christ, care for one another, and reach our city together.

“They devoted themselves to the apostles’ teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer.” – Acts 2:42

Cell Groups

Cell groups are the heartbeat of fellowship at River of Life Christian Church. These small groups meet across different parts of the city and online to study the Word, pray together, and support one another.

  • • Word-focused Bible study and discussion.
  • • Prayer for personal, family, and city needs.
  • • Fellowship over tea, snacks, and conversations.
  • • Opportunities to serve and grow in spiritual gifts.

Cell meetings typically run mid-week (see Services page). Exact locations and timings can be confirmed with the church office.

We would love to help you connect to a cell based on your area, language, and stage of life.

Fellowships for Every Season of Life

In addition to cell groups, we have focused fellowships where people in similar life stages can grow together.

⚡

Youth Fellowship

Teens & young adults

A dynamic space for youth and young adults to encounter God, build strong friendships, and be equipped to stand for Christ in schools, colleges, and the workplace.

Sessions include worship, interactive teaching, testimonies, and practical life discussions.

🌸

Women's Fellowship

Women of all ages

A safe, faith-filled community where women encourage one another, study the Word, and pray together for families, workplaces, and the nation.

Includes times of teaching, testimonies, and specific ministry for women.

🛡️

Men's Fellowship

Men, husbands, fathers

A place for men to be strengthened in their walk with God, grow as Christ-like leaders, and encourage one another in faith.

Focus on integrity, spiritual leadership, and practical discipleship.

🌐

Online Fellowship

For those joining from a distance

If you are unable to attend in person due to distance or special circumstances, we have online fellowship options so you can still stay connected.

Includes online cell groups and selected ministry meetings over video platforms.
