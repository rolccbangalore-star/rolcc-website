Giving

Worshiping God Through Generous Giving

Giving is an act of worship and trust. As we give joyfully and generously, we partner with what God is doing through River of Life Christian Church in Bangalore and beyond.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.”

– 2 Corinthians 9:7

Why We Give

We do not give to earn God's love – we give because we have already received His amazing grace in Jesus Christ. Our finances are one way we honor God, serve others, and advance the gospel.

  • • Giving is an act of worship and obedience to God.
  • • Giving reflects our trust that God is our Provider.
  • • Giving helps support ministry, missions, and mercy outreach.

Scriptures to meditate on: 2 Corinthians 9:6–11, Malachi 3:10, Proverbs 3:9–10.

A Heart Posture, Not Just a Transaction

As a church, we encourage each person to give prayerfully, joyfully, and intentionally — in line with God's leading and His Word.

If you are new or visiting, please feel no obligation to give. Your presence is a gift to us.
