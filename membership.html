How We Support You

We are here to help you and your family through life's milestones. Contact us to arrange any of the following.

Baptism

Public declaration of faith in Christ. We would love to walk with you through baptism preparation and the ceremony.

Wedding

Church weddings and ceremonies. We are honored to support couples as they prepare for marriage and celebrate their union.

Anniversaries

Celebrating milestones together. Whether it's a wedding anniversary or another special occasion, we are here to celebrate with you.

Funeral

Pastoral care and support during loss. We offer funeral services and memorial arrangements with compassion and dignity.
