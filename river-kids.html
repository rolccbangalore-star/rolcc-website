River Kids

A Joyful Place for Children to Encounter Jesus

River Kids is the children's ministry of River of Life Christian Church – a vibrant, fun, and faith-filled space where kids discover God's love and grow in His Word.

Children are a gift from the Lord.

At River Kids, we partner with parents to disciple children in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our Vision for Children

We believe children are not the church of tomorrow – they are a vital part of the church today. Our desire is to see every child:

  • • Encounter Jesus personally.
  • • Understand the Bible in a simple, age-appropriate way.
  • • Be filled with the Holy Spirit and learn to pray.
  • • Grow in godly character and Christlike kindness.
  • • Discover their gifts and use them for God's glory.

“Let the little children come to me and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” – Matthew 19:14

Age Groups

  • River Tots (Ages 3–5) – Short Bible stories, songs, and simple crafts.
  • River Explorers (Ages 6–9) – Engaging Bible lessons, memory verses, and group activities.
  • River Champions (Ages 10–12) – Deeper Bible study, discussions, and leadership-focused activities.

Age brackets may be adjusted slightly based on the number of children and specific ministry needs.

What Happens at River Kids?

River Kids combines fun, creativity, and solid Bible teaching to help children love God and love people.

  • • Interactive Bible lessons and stories.
  • • Worship, action songs, and creative expression.
  • • Scripture memory and simple faith declarations.
  • • Games, crafts, and team-building activities.
  • • Prayer time and listening to God's voice.

Sessions typically run parallel to the main Sunday service.

Our Safety Commitment

We take the safety and well-being of every child seriously.

  • • Children are always supervised by trusted teachers and volunteers.
  • • Check-in and check-out processes help ensure safe handover.
  • • Age-appropriate class sizes for better attention and care.
  • • Clean, child-friendly environments.

Parents are welcome to speak to our River Kids team before or after any service to understand how we care for children.
