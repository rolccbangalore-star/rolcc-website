River Kids is the children's ministry of River of Life Christian Church – a vibrant, fun, and faith-filled space where kids discover God's love and grow in His Word.

Visit River Kids

What We Do

River Kids combines fun, creativity, and solid Bible teaching to help children love God and love people. We believe children are not the church of tomorrow – they are a vital part of the church today.

  • Praise & Worship and Bible stories in age-appropriate ways
  • Memory verse recitals and craft days
  • Movie days and Spl Scholarship exams
  • Outreach to families of parents once in 2 months
  • Safe, supervised environments at 2 locations
