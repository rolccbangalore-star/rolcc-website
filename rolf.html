What We Do

River of Life Foundation (ROLF) serves children and families in need through a range of initiatives designed to provide practical help, educational support, and hope in difficult times.

  • Scholarship exams — supporting children's education
  • Academic training — extra help for students who need it
  • Big Box — seasonal outreach such as Christmas gift boxes
  • Package of Help — practical support for families in crisis
  • Year-round initiatives across Bengaluru
