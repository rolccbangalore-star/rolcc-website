What We Do

Every gathering at River of Life is an opportunity to encounter God, grow in His Word, and build genuine community. Whether it's Sunday worship, midweek fellowship, or a special prayer evening — we come together to be transformed.

  • Spirit-led worship and Christ-centered preaching every Sunday
  • Midweek cell groups for deeper study, prayer, and accountability
  • Children's ministry running in parallel during Sunday services
  • Special worship evenings and monthly communion services
  • Community outreach and serving opportunities throughout the year
