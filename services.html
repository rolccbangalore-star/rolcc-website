Services

Worship, Word & Fellowship Each Week

Our gatherings are centered on the presence of God, the preaching of His Word, and meaningful community. Here's how a typical week at River of Life Christian Church looks.

Sunday Service · 10:00 AM – 11:30 AM

3rd floor, AMR PLAZA, 49, 18th Main Rd, Sector 3, HSR Layout, Bengaluru.

Sunday Worship Service

Our main Sunday worship service is a time of passionate praise, Spirit-led worship, and Christ-centered preaching. All are welcome.

  • • Time: 10:00 AM – 11:30 AM
  • • Location: HSR Layout, Bengaluru (see Contact page for map)
  • • Language: Primarily English
  • • Children: River Kids ministry runs in parallel for children.

Worship Flow (Typical Order)

  1. 1. Call to worship & opening prayer
  2. 2. Praise & worship (songs of exaltation, thanksgiving, and surrender)
  3. 3. Scripture reading & exhortation
  4. 4. Message from the Word of God
  5. 5. Ministry time (prayer, response, and altar ministry as led)
  6. 6. Announcements, offering, and benediction

What to Expect

  • • Warm, welcoming atmosphere for individuals and families.
  • • Contemporary worship with a strong focus on exalting Jesus.
  • • Clear, Bible-based preaching with practical application.
  • • Opportunities for personal prayer and ministry.
